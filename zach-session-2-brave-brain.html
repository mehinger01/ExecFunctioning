Reset Toolbox

A reset is not quitting. A reset helps your brain come back.
🐢

Turtle Reset

Use when: your body feels tight, slow, or stuck.

  1. Pull your arms in like a turtle.
  2. Take one slow breath.
  3. Come back out slowly.
  4. Say: “One tiny step.”
🌪️

Shake-Out Reset

Use when: your body has too much energy or wants to escape.

  1. Shake your hands 5 times.
  2. Shake your shoulders 5 times.
  3. Stomp softly 3 times.
  4. Point to the next tiny step.
🎈

Balloon Breath

Use when: your brain feels noisy or rushed.

  1. Put hands on belly.
  2. Breathe in like filling a balloon.
  3. Breathe out slowly.
  4. Try again, smaller.
